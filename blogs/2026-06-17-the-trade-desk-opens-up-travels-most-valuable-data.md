The Trade Desk expanded its travel and hospitality commerce media network, making high-intent travel data from partners like Booking.com and Marriott Media available to more advertisers. Jeff Daniel, GM of Retail Data Partnerships, said the platform extends commerce media capabilities beyond traditional retail into the travel sector.
